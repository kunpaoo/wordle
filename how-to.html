Guess the Filipino word in 5 tries!

Each guess must be a 5-letter word.
The color of the tiles will change to show how close you are to guessing the word.

GREEN means the letter is in the word and in the correct spot.
YELLOW means the letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
GRAY means the letter is not in the word in any spot.

Example:

The word generated by the program to be guessed by the user was SULAT. The user submitted BAKIT. The game showed that the letter A in BAKIT is in the word SULAT but on a different position. It also showed that the letter T in BAKIT is in the word SULAT on the same position.
